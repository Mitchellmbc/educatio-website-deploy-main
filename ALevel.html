A-Level Tuition

Information for you...

A-Levels are some of the most difficult exams that students will ever sit, and there is immense pressure on pupils to perform well and self-study. With many A-Levels, in particular Biology, Chemistry, Maths and Physics, schools do not have enough time to cover all of the content and do enough exam practice. This leads to schools pushing pupils to study content at home. While this does prepare them for the university dynamic, this is the first time most pupils will have to teach themselves content. So what does this all mean? Well, students every year report the following concerns:


  1. Lack of exam preparation
  2. Not fully understanding the content covered at school and independently
  3. The jump in difficulty between GCSEs and A-Levels being huge
  4. Stress and anxiety over UCAS applications, predicted grades and meeting the university requirements

How We Can Help!

1-1 & Group Tuition

We offer 1-1 private tuition at your home and online at highly competitive rates. Pupils actually benefit from working in small groups with a tutor, as they compete with each other and feel less pressure as the attention is more divided, as such, we also offer group tuition of no more than 5 pupils per class at schools local to you!

Personalised Learning Plans

After returning your child's assessment to us, our experienced tutors will begin crafting a learning plan, tailor-made to your child's needs, abilities and goals! Our tutors are also extremely adaptable, and will adjust the plan should your goals or circumstances change. We constantly edit our own materials to ensure every piece of work is tailored to the individual class/pupil's needs.

Exam Preparation and Mock Testing

Our tutors provide regular testing, with a score tracker provided to each pupil so they can track their progress and regular feedback sessions to ensure your child is fully prepared for their exams and tests.

Detailed Feedback

You will have regular contact and updates regarding your child's progress. Your child will be provided detailed feedback on all of their answers with full-worked solutions, to ensure they know exactly what they need to do to achieve their goals!

WHAT DO WE COVER?

We cover Maths and Physics for Edexcel, AQA and OCR, with Chemistry and Biology for the same exam boards available on request. All of the material that we produce is designed to cover their respective specifications, and contains the most up-to-date exam questions to provide the best preparation possible.
