Our Mission

After working in schools and tuition centres for years, we were unsatisfied with a lot of the resources being sold and used online. What's worse is that tutors were also making the same mistake in their teaching.

With many tutors and resources, an X is placed next to the wrong answer and the correct one is provided with little to no explanation as to why the pupils' answer was wrong or why the provided answer was correct.

Time Consuming

The issue with this is that people learn from their mistakes only AFTER they realise what their mistake was, and why it was a mistake in the first place!

Feedback over years

Over the years, we have heard complaints from pupils and parents regarding just being provided with the right answers with no context or explanation.

So we decided to change that

Here's how we prevent this mistake

Video or live worked solutions

All of our resources have worked solutions and video solutions provided by a professional tutor, and if you're still unsure, you can book a video call with a tutor who will go through the question until you are 100% satisfied, guaranteed.

Regular Testing

All of our tuition sessions will involve full worked solutions where the pupil will be able to ask any questions they may have. Regular testing is utilised to ensure you are on the right track and making the expected progress!

Up to Date Resources

All of our products are made 100% by us, ensuring the highest quality and adaptability, and cross-checked by various tutors to ensure they are the best on the market.
